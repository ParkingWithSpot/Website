How It Works

Everything You Need — In One Parking App

Spot brings every part of parking into one seamless experience. From discovering nearby spaces to verifying street parking and paying for garages, Spot helps you park smarter, faster, and stress-free.

Smart Search

Search any location or use your current position to find available parking spaces nearby.

Add & Verify Spots

Help the community grow! Drop pins for parking garages, lots, or street zones or verify existing ones to keep the map accurate.

Payments & Links

Access partner links to pay for parking or navigate directly to garage websites.

Parking Availability Indicator

A simple crowdsourced signal for whether a spot or area is currently available.

Community-Powered

Benefit from real-time updates from other drivers about parking availability and pricing.

Gamified Rewards

Earn points for adding or verifying spots, unlock badges and rank up as a “Spot Scout.
